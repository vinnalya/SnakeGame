Snake Game Controls

Movement:

- Use the Arrow Keys to move the snake
 in the desired direction.

Special Action:

- Press the Spacebar to grow the snake by one segment.

  Note: Using the Spacebar to grow the snake will not
 add to your score.

Objective:

- Guide the snake to eat the food and grow as long as
possible without colliding with the walls or yourself.

- The longer the snake grows, the higher your score!

Good luck and have fun!
